# Gift Subscriptions

> **Phase:** 4 — Social and Household Utility  
> **Target release:** Year 1 expansion release (March 19, 2027)  
> **Milestone:** Year 1 expansion release (March 19, 2027)  
> **Tier:** Pro Annual  
> **Priority:** Low

---

## Summary

Allow users to purchase a Pro Annual subscription as a gift for someone else. Gift subscriptions are a seasonal revenue driver (holidays, birthdays, Mother's Day) and an organic acquisition channel — every gift recipient is a new user.

---

## Value Proposition

- **For users:** Give the gift of organized cooking. Perfect for the home cook in your life who is always battling recipe blogs. A thoughtful, practical gift that keeps giving all year.
- **For the business:** Gift subscriptions are incremental revenue — the buyer is often already a user, and the recipient is a net-new user. They drive acquisition with zero CAC (cost per acquired user). Holiday gift subs can spike Q4 revenue.
- **Revenue estimate:** $5,000–$15,000 in Year 1 gift subscription revenue (per the operating plan).

---

## Detailed Instructions

### 1. Gift purchase flow

- Add a "Gift Pro" option in the app settings or pricing page.
- Buyer enters: recipient's name, recipient's email, optional personal message, and desired start date (immediate or scheduled for a specific date).
- Buyer pays the Pro Annual price ($39.99) through the standard payment flow.
- A gift code is generated and delivered to the recipient via email (and optionally via a shareable link).

### 2. Gift redemption

- Recipient receives an email with the gift details, personal message, and a "Redeem" button/link.
- Clicking "Redeem" opens the app (or web) and prompts sign-up or sign-in.
- On sign-in, the Pro Annual entitlement is activated for 12 months.
- If the recipient already has an active subscription, the gift extends their subscription by 12 months.

### 3. Gift management

- Buyer can view their sent gifts and status (redeemed or pending) in their account settings.
- Allow the buyer to resend the gift email if the recipient hasn't redeemed.
- Gift codes expire after 6 months if unredeemed.

### 4. Seasonal promotion

- Create themed gift cards / emails for holidays (Christmas, Mother's Day, Father's Day, Thanksgiving).
- Optional: physical gift card design (PDF printable) for in-person gifting.

### 5. RevenueCat integration

- Gift subscriptions should be tracked as a separate offering in RevenueCat.
- Ensure gift entitlements are correctly reflected in the backend and sync across platforms.
